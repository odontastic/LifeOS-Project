## LifeOS – The Human Operating System
**Version:** 1.0
**Tagline:** *Master your mind. Deepen your relationships. Build a life with virtue and clarity.*

***

### 1. Vision: The Return of the Human Center

LifeOS exists to bridge two worlds — *human emotional intelligence* and *technological precision*.

It’s more than a productivity tool; it’s an operating system for living — one that helps you integrate your **tasks**, **knowledge**, **emotions**, and **relationships** into one calm, intelligent flow.

**Core Philosophy:**
- **Virtue → Connection → Clarity → Embodiment.**
  Every design choice serves to strengthen the user’s capacity to act with integrity, empathy, and awareness.
- **Emotional Intelligence as Executive Function.**
  LifeOS operationalizes concepts from CBT, ACT, Stoicism, and Emotional Granularity research (Feldman Barrett, Goleman, Kabat-Zinn).
- **From Data to Insight, from Insight to Love.**
  The goal is not collecting information but transforming it into alignment — with your values, your people, your purpose.

***

### 2. The Pact with the User
**Our foundational UX covenant:** respect human dignity, never manipulate attention, never coerce engagement.

#### Principles
1. **Frictionless when stressed, deep when calm.**
   One-click reflection during chaos; layered analysis when ready.
2. **Privacy-first and user-sovereign.**
   Local data by default. Your emotions never become analytics for others.
3. **Emotional Safety by Design.**
   Language, color, pacing, and prompts designed not to judge or shame.
4. **Human before Machine.**
   AI is a companion — not your replacement, not your therapist. LifeOS is a mirror and a mentor, not a master.

### 6. Core Modules: The Ecosystem of Attention

#### A. **Inner Palette – Feel What You Feel**
**Purpose:** Real-time mood, body, and context awareness.
**Benefit:** Builds emotional literacy and self-regulation.

**Features:**
- Interactive Plutchik Wheel: Tap feelings, see color-coded affective matrix.
- Somatic Map: Body heatmap logs sensations.
- Valence/Arousal sliders: Quantify state for trend analytics.
- Smart Prompting: Contextual reflections — *“What changed right before you felt this?”*
- Adaptive Feedback Loop: Recommends Calm Compass exercises or reflection in Prism Clarity Studio.

**Scientifically Supported Outcomes:**
Emotional labeling reduces amygdala activation (Lieberman et al., 2007) — promoting calm and cognitive flexibility.

***

#### B. **Connection Engine – Remember Love**
**Purpose:** Transform good intentions into consistent care.
**Benefit:** Strengthens empathy, continuity, and social memory.

**Features:**
- Relationship Profiles with Open Loops: Track what people say or need (“Mom’s knee hurting,” “Son’s science fair coming up”).
- Memory Prosthesis: Summaries of last 3-5 interactions with sentiment analysis.
- Loop Closer: Prompts you to follow up — “Ask John how the exam went.”
- Contextual Chat: Every conversation supported by a reflective AI agent helping you phrase messages authentically.
- Evening Examen Integration: Family/prayer check-ins attached to your nightly reflection ritual.

**Differentiator:**
Instead of replacing human connection with notifications, LifeOS restores *memory-based presence*. It doesn’t ping; it *reminds you why you care.*

***

#### C. **Prism Clarity Studio – Think with Precision**
**Purpose:** Emotional intelligence meets mental model synthesis.
**Benefit:** Clearer reasoning, virtue alignment, and values-driven choices.

**Features:**
- Decision Matrices & Value Checks.
- Automatic Bias Detection (All-or-Nothing, Catastrophizing, Mind Reading).
- Personalized Mental Models Library (Inversion, First Principles, Bayesian Updating).
- Narrative Analyzer for journaling.
- “Voice of Counsel” mode that generates compassionate CBT-style reframes.

**Supported by:**
Kahneman’s dual-process theory and Beck’s cognitive triad — helping users balance reason and emotion.

***

#### D. **Calm Compass – Ground and Regroup**
**Purpose:** Moments of emotional overwhelm need simplicity, not complexity.
**Benefit:** Quick regulation and self-compassion in under 60 seconds.

**Modes:**
1. **Breathe:** Cyclic breath patterns with animation & audio guidance.
2. **Connect:** One-tap link to trusted family or support people.
3. **Reflect Later:** Log emotion silently for later unpacking.

**Evidence Base:**
Incorporates Jon Kabat-Zinn’s mindfulness and polyvagal-informed grounding exercises.

***

#### E. **The Engine Room – Productivity Made Human**
**Purpose:** Reduce anxiety by aligning tasks with current energy and emotional state.
**Benefit:** Sustainable productivity through emotional alignment.

**Features:**
- PARA & GTD hybrid task sorting (Projects/Areas/Resources/Archives).
- Emotion-linked Task Visibility: High arousal → focus on calming or mechanical tasks only.
- Weekly Review Rituals syncing cognitive, emotional, and relational data into one reflection.
- Optional integrations (calendar, email parsing, or wearables).

**Outcome:**
Restores trust in your commitments — clear mind, meaningful action.

### 7. UX & UI: The Emotional Interface
**Design Language:** *Monastic Industrial Minimalism.*
Form meets tranquility — matte gradients, legible typography, gentle contrast.

**Core UX Tenets:**
- Every element communicates calm and care.
- Fast capture, slow reflection.
- Progressive disclosure: depth only when invited.
- Natural language chat for intuitive navigation: “Show me how I felt most evenings this week” or “Remind me what I promised Natalie.”

**Cross-Module Synergy:**
Emotions link to tasks. Tasks link to people. People link to meaning. Everything interconnects in an **emotion-aware knowledge graph**.

**AI Augmentation:**
Local Llama3 or cloud optional GPT interface provides:
- Reflective summaries of your day.
- Compassionate conversational check-ins.
- Idea synthesis from Zettelkasten insights and emotional trends.
