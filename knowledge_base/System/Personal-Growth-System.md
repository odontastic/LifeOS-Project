# Personal Growth & Alignment System

This system is designed to manage ongoing processes, emotional habits, and long-term self-improvement. It shifts focus from "completing tasks" to "reviewing alignment" with your desired identity.

## 1. Core Domains (The "Swimlanes")
These domains categorize your efforts and ensure balance.
1.  **Family & Relationships**: Wife, Sons, Extended Family.
2.  **Healthy Aging & Longevity**: Strategic health choices, research, long-term planning.
3.  **Wellness**: Day-to-day physical & mental health (exercise, diet, mindfulness).
4.  **Life Admin**: Necessary discrete tasks enabling main goals.

## 2. Data Structure (The "Columns")

### A. Guiding Principles (Identity)
*Static "State of Being" goals. Your compass.*
*   **Location**: `System/Guiding-Principles.md`
*   **Format**: List of affirmation-style statements.
*   **Examples**: "I am a patient and present father.", "I make disciplined choices for long-term health."

### B. Long-Term Outcomes (1-5 Years)
*Tangible signs that you are living your principles.*
*   **Location**: `Areas/Goals/Long-Term-Outcomes.md`
*   **Format**: Bulleted list per Domain.
*   **Examples**: "Sons voluntarily share personal challenges.", "VO2 max in top 10% for age group."

### C. Quarterly Initiatives (90 Days)
*Major processes or projects to establish.*
*   **Location**: `Areas/Goals/Quarterly-Initiatives.md`
*   **Format**: Actionable projects linked to Outcomes.
*   **Examples**: "Establish weekly screen-free activity with sons.", "Complete longevity consultation."

### D. Weekly Review Prompts (The Engine)
*Recurring reflection questions derived from Initiatives.*
*   **Location**: `System/Reviews/Weekly-Prompts.md`
*   **Format**: Checklist of questions/prompts.
*   **Examples**: "Review: Family Presence & Connection", "Review: Adherence to Longevity Protocol"

### E. Insights & Learnings
*The output of your reviews.*
*   **Location**: `Areas/Personal-Reflection/Journal/` (Daily/Weekly entries)
*   **Format**: "Insight: [Observation]. Goal: [Adjustment]."

## 4. The Workflow (Cadence)

### Short-Term: Daily Intention (5 Minutes)
*   **Trigger**: Morning Calibration.
*   **Action**: Read `System/Guiding-Principles.md`. Pick one to embody today.

### Medium-Term: Weekly Review (30-45 Minutes)
*   **Trigger**: Weekend Review session.
*   **Action**:
    1.  **Reflect**: Select 1-2 prompts from `Weekly-Prompts.md`.
    2.  **Journal**: Answer them in your Weekly Journal entry.
    3.  **Capture**: Record key insights and adjustments.

### Long-Term: Quarterly Retreat (2-3 Hours)
*   **Trigger**: End of Quarter.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Review Journal insights from the quarter.
    2.  Assess progress on Long-Term Outcomes.
    3.  Define new Quarterly Initiatives.
    4.  Update Weekly Prompts.
