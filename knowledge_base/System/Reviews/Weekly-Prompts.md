# Weekly Review Prompts

> **Purpose**: The engine of the system. Select 1-2 of these prompts during your Weekly Review to assess alignment.

## Family Presence
- [ ] **Review: Connection**: On a scale of 1-10, how present was I with my family this week? What was the biggest distractor?
- [ ] **Review: Patience**: Did I pause before reacting to stress with the boys?

## Health & Longevity
- [ ] **Review: Protocol Adherence**: Did I stick to my supplement and exercise protocol? If not, what triggered the slip?
- [ ] **Review: Sleep**: How was my sleep quality? What variables affected it?

## Mental Wellness
- [ ] **Review: Stress Response**: What was the most stressful moment? How did I handle it? (Mindfulness vs. Reactivity)

## General
- [ ] **Review: The One Thing**: Did I make progress on my Quarterly Initiatives?
