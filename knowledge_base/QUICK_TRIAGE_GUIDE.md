## 1. THE DECISION MATRIX

**Step 1: Is it Actionable?**
*   **NO**:
    *   **Trash?** -> DELETE.
    *   **Someday/Maybe?** -> ARCHIVE (or specific list).
    *   **Reference?** -> RESOURCE (File it by topic).
*   **YES**:
    *   **< 2 mins?** -> DO IT NOW.
    *   **Delegate?** -> WAITING FOR (Tag person).
    *   **Defer?** -> CALENDAR (Time specific) or NEXT ACTIONS (Asap).

## 2. THE PARA SORTING HAT

### üèóÔ∏è PROJECT (Goal)
*   **Definition**: A series of tasks linked to a **goal** with a **deadline**.
*   *Examples*: "Finish Report", "Plan Vacation", "Buy Car".
*   *Action*: Create a folder/file in `Projects/`.

### üèîÔ∏è AREA (Standard)
*   **Definition**: An activity with a **standard to maintain** over time. No end date.
*   *Examples*: "Health", "Finances", "Professional Development", "Car Maintenance".
*   *Action*: Append to the relevant file in `Areas/`.

### üìö RESOURCE (Topic)
*   **Definition**: A topic of **ongoing interest** or utility.
*   *Examples*: "Cooking", "Marketing", "History", "Book Notes".
*   *Action*: File in `Resources/`.

### üì¶ ARCHIVE (Inactive)
*   **Definition**: Inactive items from the other 3 categories.
*   *Examples*: "Completed Projects", "Past Year Taxes".
*   *Action*: Move to `Archives/`.
