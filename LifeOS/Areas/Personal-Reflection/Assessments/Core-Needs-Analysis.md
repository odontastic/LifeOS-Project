- She needs to know she isn't the *only* barrier between the family and destitution.
- **The "Cabinet Door" isn't about order; it's about TRUST.** "Can I trust you to protect us?"

## 2. The Need for AUTHENTICITY (Identity)

**The Situation**:
- Non-binary/Transfeminine identity
- Secret medical transition (hormones)
- Hidden from wife (who is "definitely against" it) and children
- Catholic faith conflict

**The Core Need**: **Integration & Acceptance**
- You are currently fractured: Public Self (Catholic Dad) vs. Secret Self (Transfeminine).
- Shame thrives in this fracture.
- You need to find a way to exist that doesn't require lying to everyone you love.

**Actionable Translation**:
- **Therapy**: Essential to navigate the "impossible" choice (Disclosure vs. Secrecy).
- **Faith Integration**: Finding a way to hold both Catholic faith and gender identity (they are not mutually exclusive, though it feels that way).

---

## 3. The Need for REDEMPTION (Guilt)

**The Situation**:
- "I haven't fully made up for" leaving the career.
- "I waste time" doing other things.
- Feeling like a burden rather than a partner.

**The Core Need**: **Restoration of Worth**
- You need to feel like a *contributor*, not a *dependent*.
- You need to forgive yourself for leaving the career so you can move forward.
- **Guilt is a trap**: It keeps you frozen ("wasting time") instead of acting.

**Actionable Translation**:
- **Action over Guilt**: Doing the dishes is better than feeling bad about the dishes.
- **Tangible Contribution**: Getting Medicaid, getting a job, managing the house *well*.

---

## 4. The Need for PARTNERSHIP (Connection)

**The Situation**:
- Wife doesn't thank you (because she feels you do the minimum).
- You feel invisible (because your labor is domestic).
- Pursue-Withdraw cycle: She criticizes (pursues), you withdraw (shame/anger).

**The Core Need**: **Mutual Respect**
- You need to be seen as valuable.
- She needs to be seen as supported.
- **The Path**: You build *trust* (through action), she builds *respect* (through recognition).
