## ðŸ§˜ Personal Protocol (The "Dad" Stack)
**Focus**: Mobility, Joint Health, Functional Strength.

### 1. Mobility First (Daily - 15 mins)
*   **Tools**: Yoga Mat, Foam Roller.
*   **App**: **GOWOD** (Mobility for functional fitness) or **Down Dog Yoga** (Customizable).
*   **Routine**:
    *   Morning: 5-min "Grease the Groove" (Joint circles, cat-cow).
    *   Evening: 10-min Decompression (Hip openers, thoracic spine).

### 2. Strength (3x Week - 30 mins)
*   **Equipment**: Kettlebell, Dumbbells, Bands.
*   **Method**: Minimal Effective Dose.
    *   *Kettlebell Swings*: The king of posterior chain (back/glutes) health.
    *   *Goblet Squats*: Hip mobility + leg strength.
    *   *Pushups/Band Pull-aparts*: Upper body push/pull balance.
    *   *Carries*: Farmer walks for core stability.

### 3. Bio-Data Tracking
*   **Google Fit**: Central hub.
*   **HRV (Heart Rate Variability)**: Use camera-based app (e.g., Welltory) to measure stress/recovery status each morning.
