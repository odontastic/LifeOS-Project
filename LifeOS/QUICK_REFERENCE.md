## üìã What I Can Do (By Category)

### 1. DAILY COACHING & ACCOUNTABILITY

**Morning Calibration**
- Help you select your ONE THING for the day
- Check emotional state and wife's state
- Set virtue focus for the day
- **Command**: `[Daily]` or "What should my ONE THING be today?"

**Evening Reflection**
- Review if you completed the ONE THING
- Process relationship interactions
- Log emotions and wins
- Plan tomorrow's priority
- **Command**: "Evening reflection" or "Let's review my day"

**Emotion Check-Ins**
- Name and process current emotions
- Practice perception checking
- Track emotional patterns
- **Command**: "I'm feeling [emotion]" or "Help me understand what I'm feeling"

### 2. RELATIONSHIP COACHING

**Crisis De-Escalation**
- Real-time support during conflict with wife
- First-Aid Kit phrase suggestions
- Stay-in-room coaching
- **Command**: `[Crisis]` or "I'm in a fight with my wife"

**Pattern Analysis**
- Identify pursue-withdraw cycle triggers
- Map shame-avoidance loops
- Track "small things" that signal bigger issues
- **Command**: "Analyze my relationship patterns" or "Why do we keep fighting about [X]?"

**Repair Guidance**
- Help craft repair attempts after conflict
- Choose appropriate vulnerability level
- Time repair attempts
- **Command**: "Help me repair after last night's fight"

**Wife Translator**
- Decode her "high standards" as safety needs
- Reframe criticism as fear/anxiety
- Understand her perspective
- **Command**: "Why does she care so much about [X]?" or "Translate this conflict"

### 3. EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE DEVELOPMENT

**Emotion Labeling Practice**
- Build emotional vocabulary
- Use emotion wheel
- Track emotional granularity over time
- **Command**: "Help me name this feeling" or "Emotion practice"

**Perception Checking**
- Practice reading wife's emotional state
- Learn to ask instead of assume
- Build empathy skills
- **Command**: "How do I check if she's feeling [X]?"

**Shame Resilience**
- Identify shame triggers
- Interrupt shame-withdrawal loop
- Practice self-compassion
- **Command**: "I'm feeling ashamed about [X]" or "Shame work"

### 4. TASK & PROJECT MANAGEMENT (GTD)

**Capture & Process**
- Add tasks to inbox
- Clarify next actions
- Organize by context, energy, time
- **Command**: "Add task: [description]" or "Process my inbox"

**Weekly Review**
- Clear all inboxes
- Review active projects
- Check waiting-for items
- Plan next week
- **Command**: `[Review]` or "Weekly review"

**Context Views**
- Show tasks by context (@Home, @Computer, etc.)
- Filter by energy level
- Quick wins (5-15 min tasks)
- **Command**: "Show me @Home tasks" or "What can I do in 15 minutes?"

### 5. KNOWLEDGE MANAGEMENT

**Note Creation**
- Create properly formatted notes with metadata
- Link to projects/areas
- Add to MOCs
- **Command**: "Create note about [topic]"

**Session Import**
- Import past LLM conversations
- Extract insights and action items
- Link to relevant projects
- **Command**: "Import session from [date]" + paste conversation

**Pattern Recognition**
- Analyze notes for recurring themes
- Identify blind spots
- Track growth over time
- **Command**: "What patterns do you see in my notes?"

**MOC Creation**
- Build Maps of Content for topics
- Connect related notes
- Create knowledge hubs
- **Command**: "Create MOC for [topic]"

### 6. HABIT TRACKING & FORMATION

**Habit Setup**
- Design micro-habits
- Create implementation intentions
- Set up tracking
- **Command**: "Help me build habit: [description]"

**Progress Tracking**
- Log daily completion
- Calculate streaks
- Identify obstacles
- **Command**: "Track habit: [name]" or "Show my streaks"

**Behavior Change**
- Apply Atomic Habits framework
- Design environment
- Stack habits
- **Command**: "How do I make [habit] stick?"

### 7. SPIRITUAL INTEGRATION

**Daily Examen**
- Ignatian reflection practice
- Gratitude, review, sorrow, forgiveness, grace
- **Command**: "Examen" or "Evening prayer reflection"

**Virtue Development**
- Identify target virtues
- Create practice plan
- Track growth
- **Command**: "Help me practice [virtue]"

**Confession Prep**
- Examine conscience
- Identify patterns of sin
- Prepare for sacrament
- **Command**: "Confession preparation"

### 8. ASSESSMENT & ANALYSIS

**Psychological Assessment**
- Deep pattern analysis
- Attachment style insights
- Cognitive distortion identification
- **Command**: `[Assess]` or "Assess my [area]"

**Progress Review**
- Compare current vs. baseline
- Identify growth areas
- Celebrate wins
- **Command**: "Show my progress on [goal]"

**Blind Spot Detection**
- Identify what you're missing
- Challenge assumptions
- Reveal patterns
- **Command**: "What am I not seeing about [situation]?"

## üí° Tips for Working With Me

### 1. Be Specific
‚ùå "Help me with my marriage"
‚úÖ "My wife got upset about the kitchen. Help me understand why and how to respond."

### 2. Use Commands
The bracketed commands `[Daily]`, `[Review]`, `[Crisis]` trigger specific workflows

### 3. Tell Me Your Constraints
- Time available
- Energy level
- Context (@Home, @Computer, etc.)
- Emotional state

### 4. Ask for Clarification
If my response isn't helpful, say:
- "Can you be more specific?"
- "Give me an example"
- "Make this simpler"

### 5. Challenge Me
If something doesn't feel right:
- "I don't think that's accurate"
- "That doesn't match my experience"
- "Help me understand the reasoning"
