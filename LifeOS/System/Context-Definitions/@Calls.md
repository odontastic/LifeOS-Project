# @Calls Context

## Description
Actions that require phone calls or voice communication, including consultations, inquiries, and verbal coordination.

## Typical Activities
- Phone consultations and inquiries
- Customer service calls
- Professional consultations
- Coordination with service providers
- Information gathering via phone

## Related Projects
- College Financial Aid Research (contacting financial aid offices)
- Various service-related projects requiring phone coordination

## Equipment Needed
- Phone or mobile device
- Quiet calling environment
- Contact information and notes
- Calendar for scheduling calls

## Best Time to Execute
- During business hours for professional calls
- When dedicated calling time is available
- When phone-based research or coordination is needed
