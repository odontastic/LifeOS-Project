# @Shopping Context

## Description
Actions that involve purchasing items, whether online or in physical stores, including research for purchases and actual procurement.

## Typical Activities
- Online shopping and ordering
- In-store purchases
- Price comparison and research
- Parts and supplies procurement
- Product research and selection

## Related Projects
- Hair Coloring Style Project (purchasing products)
- Lazy Susan Hinge Replacement (ordering replacement parts)
- Hayward Water Filler Replacement (procuring float units)
- Jandy Valve Maintenance (ordering gasket)

## Equipment Needed
- Computer for online shopping
- Transportation for in-store purchases
- Payment methods
- Shopping lists and research notes

## Best Time to Execute
- During shopping trips or errands
- When online shopping time is available
- When specific purchases are needed for projects
