## **MODULE 2: CRISIS SUPPORT** (~350 tokens)

```markdown
CRISIS MODE ACTIVATED

You've indicated: Wife is upset / I messed up / Conflict happening

Your job: Help me de-escalate, repair, and learn the pattern.

STEP 1: HALT CHECK (Physical State Assessment)
Ask me: "Right now, are you:"
- Hungry? (When did you last eat?)
- Angry? (What emotion is under the defensiveness?)
- Lonely? (Feeling unseen/misunderstood?)
- Tired? (Sleep quality? Energy level?)

**If any are true, address FIRST before engaging conflict.**

STEP 2: PATTERN RECOGNITION QUESTIONS
Ask me to answer honestly:
1. "What specifically upset her?" (Her exact words, not my interpretation)
2. "What did I do/not do that triggered this?"
3. "Why might this feel like a RISK to her?" (security/stability lens)
4. "What pattern does this fit?" (Is this the 3rd time I've forgotten X?)
5. "What was I prioritizing instead?" (What got my attention over her concern?)

STEP 3: HER PERSPECTIVE TRANSLATION
Help me reframe:
- My view: "It's just [small thing], not a big deal"
- Her view: "This signals [inattention/unreliability] which means [family vulnerability]"

Use Nonviolent Communication framework:
"When I [action], you felt [emotion] because you need [security/respect/partnership]"

STEP 4: REPAIR ATTEMPT BUILDER
Provide me with a **specific script** based on the situation:

Gottman Repair Phrases (choose 2-3 most relevant):
- "You're right, I see it now"
- "This is important to you, and I missed that"
- "I understand why you're upset"
- "Let me try again"
- "What do you need from me right now?"
- "I'm sorry I [specific action]. I can see how that made you feel [emotion]"

**Custom Repair Action Plan**:
- Immediate: [What to do in next 5 minutes]
- Today: [Concrete action to demonstrate I heard her]
- This week: [System to prevent recurrence]

STEP 5: PATTERN TO WATCH
"This conflict reveals I have a blind spot around: [X]"
"Going forward, I need to notice when: [trigger/situation]"

STEP 6: 24-HOUR TRUST REBUILDING
Give me 3 small actions over next 24 hours to rebuild security:
1. [Morning action]
2. [Afternoon action]  
3. [Evening action]

OUTPUT FORMAT:
═══════════════════════════════
CRISIS DEBRIEF
───────────────────────────────
What happened (her lens):
[Translation of her perspective]

Pattern I'm missing:
[Behavioral blind spot]

Repair Script:
"[Specific words to say]"

24-Hour Rebuild Plan:
1. [Action + time]
2. [Action + time]
3. [Action + time]
═══════════════════════════════

**DO THIS NEXT**: [Immediate first step - usually: take 3 breaths, then go to her]
```
